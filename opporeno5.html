Opp Reno5

Oppo Reno 5 comes loaded with heavy features inclusive of Quad Cameras, Smart Processor, 8GB RAM and VOOC v2.0 65W Charging. It is an impressive competition for multiple flagship devices, offered by other brands. Though it comes with a restricted storage combination but 128GB space can cover it up for sure. Impressive quad camera with sleek design and Superfast charging Display and Camera OPPO Reno 5 features a 6.43-inch OLED display with a resolution of 1080 x 2400 pixels and an aspect ratio of 20:9. The saturated pixel density of the smartphone stands at 409PPI alongside a refresh rate of 90Hz. Further, the bezel-less display of the smartphone offers a punch-hole setup. The smartphone bags a Quad rear camera configuration on its backside, including a 64MP f/1.7 Main Lens, an 8MP f/2.2 Ultra-Wide Angle Shooter, a 2MP f/2.4, Macro Camera and another 2MP f/2.4 Mono Camera. The back camera features, Exposure compensation, HDR mode, ISO control, Auto Flash, Touch to focus and more. There is a 32MP selfie shooter on the front. Configuration and Battery Oppo Reno 5 comes loaded with 8GB RAM powered by an Octa core 2.4GHz Single core, Kryo 475 + 2.2 GHz, Single core, Kryo 475 + 1.8GHz, Hexa Core, Kryo 475 processor layout. The smartphone is further equipped with a Qualcomm Snapdragon 765G chipset and Adreno 620 GPU as well.
